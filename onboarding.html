Uber Eats Setup

Follow these steps to connect your account

1
Log into Uber Eats Manager
Go to restaurants.ubereats.com and sign in with your restaurant account.
💡 Use the email you registered your restaurant with
2
Go to the Reports Section
On the left menu, click "Reports". Then click "Request Reports" in the top right corner.
💡 It looks like a bar chart icon in the menu
3
Select Report Type
Choose "Order History" as the report type. Select the date range you want (last 7 or 30 days is best).
💡 Order History shows all your product sales data
4
Download & Upload to Hungrin
When the report is ready, click "Download" to save the CSV file. Then upload it to Hungrin using the CSV Upload page.
💡 Uber Eats will also email you when your report is ready
🛵

Deliveroo Setup

Follow these steps to connect your account

1
Log into Deliveroo Restaurant Hub
Go to restaurant-hub.deliveroo.com and sign in with your restaurant account.
💡 Use the email address linked to your Deliveroo restaurant
2
Go to Analytics & Reports
Click on "Analytics" in the top navigation bar. Then select "Reports" from the dropdown menu.
💡 You will see a list of available reports here
3
Export Your Sales Report
Select "Sales Summary" report. Choose your date range and click "Export to CSV".
💡 The file will download automatically to your computer
4
Upload the CSV to Hungrin
Go to the CSV Upload page on Hungrin and upload the file you just downloaded. Hungrin will analyse it automatically!
💡 Do this weekly for best AI results
🍽️

Just Eat Setup

Follow these steps to connect your account

1
Log into Just Eat Restaurant Portal
Go to restaurant.just-eat.co.uk and sign in with your restaurant login details.
💡 This is different from the customer Just Eat app
2
Find Your Sales Reports
Click on "My Restaurant" then select "Sales & Performance" from the menu.
💡 You can see your order history and sales here
3
Download Your Report
Select your date range and click "Download Report". The file will be saved as a CSV on your computer.
💡 Choose "Last 30 days" for the best AI insights
4
Upload to Hungrin
Go to the CSV Upload page on Hungrin, select "Just Eat" tab and upload your file. Done!
💡 Hungrin will instantly analyse your sales and suggest promotions
